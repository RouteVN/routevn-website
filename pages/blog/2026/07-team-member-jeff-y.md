## Team Member Introduction: JeffY

JeffY is a second-year Computer Science student at University of New South Wales, Australia. He has experience building JavaScript systems and is interested in visual novels and visual novel tooling.
He joined RouteVN as an intern from December 2025 to March 2026.

### About JeffY

JeffY's programming journey started early, with single-chip robot programming in primary school, Scratch in junior high, and Python in senior high.

He is currently focused on growing both frontend and backend engineering skills, and wants to strengthen his ability to collaborate in a small, fast-moving team.

### Experience with Visual Novels

JeffY has read visual novels in both Chinese and English across multiple genres, including science fiction, horror, comedy, and romance.

Some of the visual novels he has played:

- Doki Doki Literature Club!
- Steins;Gate
- Chaos;Child
- Danganronpa
- Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trilogy

### Contributions at RouteVN

JeffY has contributed across RouteVN's core projects:

- **Route Graphics**: Particle system.
- **Route Engine**: Many critical features, including menu page, history mode, NVL mode, save/load functionality, and variables.
- **RouteVN Creator Client**: Next-line config, additional layout editor features, and variables page improvements.

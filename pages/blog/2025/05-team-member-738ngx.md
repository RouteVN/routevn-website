## Team Member Introduction: 738NGX

<img src="/public/blog/2025/05/738ngx-avatar.png" style="width: 200px; border-radius: 50%; margin-bottom: 24px;">

738NGX joined RouteVN as an intern in July 2025 and will finish his intern this week in December. During his time here, he has made significant contributions to RouteVN.

### About 738NGX

738NGX is a Computer Science undergraduate at SCAI, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. He is passionate about coding, and his other interests include ACGN, transportation, photography, and history.

### Experience with Visual Novels

738NGX is an avid visual novel reader since high school. He has witnessed the technical evolution of VNs, from static sprites to Live2D, and has experimented with various engines to create his own projects.

Some of his favorites:

- 高考恋爱一百天 (Gaokao Love 100 Days)
- 回忆忘却之匣 (Memory Oblivion Box)
- 真恋\~寄语枫秋\~ (True Love \~Confide to the Maple\~)
- 三色绘恋 (Tricolour Lovestory)
- 某一种青春
- 他人世界末 (On-looker)
- 饿殍：明末千里行 (The Hungry Lamb)
- ラブライブ！虹ヶ咲学園 (Love Live! Nijigasaki)

### Contributions at RouteVN

738NGX has been a core contributor to [RouteVN Creator Client](https://github.com/RouteVN/routevn-creator-client), having authored over 130+ merged PRs. He has made changes and improvements to almost all pages, and was the main person who migrated the client from a web environment into a Tauri.

Other than coding he also made significant contributions in product research and design using his experience in visual novel game mechanics and other visual novel engines.

The team is grateful for all of 738NGX's contributions during his time at RouteVN. We wish him all the best in his future adventures!

## About Yuusoft and RouteVN
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is a company incorporated in Singapore
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is the company that builds ands operates RouteVN
- RouteVN is a dedicated platform for Visual Novels
  - Our [mission](/blog/2025/01-to-fulfill-the-full-potential-of-visual-novels/) is to fulfill the full potential of Visual Novels
  - We create tools and technology to create, distribute and read Visual Novels

## Internship
- Compensation
  - 600 USD / month for the first 3 months *
  - 900 USD / month from 4th month onwards *
- Minimum duration of 3 months. Maximum duration of 6 months
- Must be able to work **full-time** during the duration of the internship
- This is a fully remote position. Applicants are welcome to apply from any timezone.

*Internships from Singapore may have different rates due to local requirements

## Job Description
- Your mandate is to work to improve the product and building features for RouteVN
- You will be working closely with [@han4wluc (Luciano)](https://han4wluc.com/) on a daily basis
- You are expected to spend 70%+ of your time on engineering aspects and the remaining 30% on product aspects.

## What an ideal candidate looks like
- You have passion for Visual Novels and you share the RouteVN vision
- You genuively enjoy coding all day
- You have a taste and high admiration for well crafted products
- You are curious, fast learner and enjoy being exposed to new technologies
- You are able to work independently take your own initiatives and resposibilities

## Tech stack
- We write almost all our code in Javascript and a little bit of Python
- We build our own frameworks and libraries from scratch and open source all of it. Including:
  - [UI Library](https://github.com/yuusoft-org/rettangoli/tree/main/packages/rettangoli-ui)
  - [Frontend framework](https://github.com/yuusoft-org/rettangoli/tree/main/packages/rettangoli-fe)
  - [Static site generator](https://github.com/yuusoft-org/rettangoli/tree/main/packages/rettangoli-sites)
  - Backend framework
  - [Graphics DSL](https://github.com/yuusoft-org/route-graphics)
  - Visual Novel Engine
  - Custom internal tools

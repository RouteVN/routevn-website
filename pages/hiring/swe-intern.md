## About Yuusoft and RouteVN
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is a company incorporated in Singapore
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is the company that builds ands operates RouteVN
- RouteVN is platform for Visual Novels
  - Read more about [the mission](/blog/2025/01-to-fulfill-the-whole-potential-of-visual-novels/)
  - Check out [demo](/creator/about/) for Visual Novel creation tool
  - Check the [vision](/blog/2025/02-building-a-visual-novel-store/) and [demo](/store) for the Visual Novel store

## Internship
- Compensation
  - 600 USD / month for the first 3 months *
  - 900 USD / month from 4th month onwards *
- Minimum duration of 3 months. Maximum duration of 6 months
- Must be able to work full-time during the duration of the internship
- This is a fully remote position. Applicants are welcome to apply from any timezone.

*Internships from Singapore may have different rates due to local requirements

## Job Description
- Your mandate is to work to improve the product and building features for RouteVN
- You will be working closely with [@han4wluc (Luciano)](https://han4wluc.com/) on a daily basis
- You are expected to be able to learn fast, work independently, and do whatever is needed to make meaningful contributions to the product

## What an ideal candidate looks like
- You have a passion for Visual Novels and you share the RouteVN vision
- You have a taste and high admiration for well designed products
- You are a fullstack software engineer, you enjoy working on frontend, but can also work on design, backend, devops, data, AI etc...

## Tech stack
- We write most of our code in Javascript
- We have built several pieces of software from scratch, including:
  - [Rettangoli](https://rettangoli.dev/): a frontend UI library
  - Graphics rendering language based on PixiJS
  - Visual Novel engine
  - Custom frontend and backend framework
- We will be integrating AI tools in places where it makes sense

## Application and selection process
- Application submission. You will be filling a form to provide information regarding the following topics:
  - About yourself
  - Your experience with Visual Novels
  - Evaluate your product sense
  - Evaluate your technical and coding skill
- Video Interviews.  We will be deep diving into different topics.
- Paid work trial. You will be working for a few days on a real world assignment.

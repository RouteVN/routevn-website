## About Yuusoft and RouteVN
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is a company incorporated in Singapore
- Yuusoft Pte. Ltd. is the company that builds and operates RouteVN
- RouteVN is a dedicated platform for Visual Novels
  - Our [mission](/blog/2025/01-to-fulfill-the-full-potential-of-visual-novels/) is to fulfil the full potential of Visual Novels
  - We build tools and technology to create, distribute and read Visual Novels

## Internship
- Compensation
  - 600 USD / month for the first 3 months *
  - 900 USD / month from 4th month onwards *
- Minimum duration of 3 months. Maximum duration of 6 months
- Must be able to work **full-time** during the duration of the internship
- This is a fully remote position. Applicants are welcome to apply from any timezone.

*Internships from Singapore may have different rates due to local requirements

## Job Description
- We're looking for a rare talent who can handle both creative writing and visual illustration
- You will be using RouteVN's product to create meaningful free-to-play Visual Novels
- Ideate and discuss with the team to come up with Visual Novel concepts
- Writing: craft compelling narratives, develop world-building elements, write engaging dialogue, and create memorable characters
- Illustration: design expressive character sprites, create immersive backgrounds, UI elements, and CG illustrations for key scenes

## What an ideal candidate looks like
- You have a genuine passion for Visual Novels and storytelling
- You genuinely enjoy writing and drawing all day
- You have a portfolio demonstrating your writing and illustration skills
- You are curious, a fast learner, and enjoy creative collaboration
- You are able to work with a team and meet deadlines
- You are able to work independently, take your own initiatives and responsibilities

## Application and selection process
- Application form submission
- Video Interview
- Take home assignment
- Paid work trial
